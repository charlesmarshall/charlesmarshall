Sorry, Tiny JS is no more...

After a great time making and looking after the site, I decided to put an end to it...

It was an experiment that worked well to start with, but even after getting featured in places like smashing magazine a community failed to develop. Probably down to my neglect...

I simply haven't had the time to sort out the niggling bugs (git syncing for example) or give it the revamp it needed. The plans were grand, but never realised.
