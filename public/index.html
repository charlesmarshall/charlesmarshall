Wildfire CMS

Our trusted CMS has been around for quite a while now, since late 2006. Back then we used subversion, and you can see the very first commit to this day on our Git repo.

Technology always moves on and just as we moved to Git from subversion, Wildfire has had its fair share of architecture changes as well. In the early days it was a very basic setup just serving blog pages, these days it powers huge sites like National Express buses & Subaru.

The new version is being polished and made ready for its production debut, but in the meantime I've scratched a nostalgic itch and put together a timeline of the project.
