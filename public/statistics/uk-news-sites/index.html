UK News

Every news outlet does its news in a different way; some are high brow intellectual works of art, others go for the lowest common denominator. But is there anything we can learn from purely the content?

I've been scanning content of various sites to produce some basic charts based on the titles; word length & title length to start with, hopefully a few more when I can.

If you want to read more about how I gather these numbers, read about it here

Word lengths

As each site has a different amount of headlines on the home page, the occurances are actually percentages. If there are 100 words & a 7 letter happens twice, that would be 2%.

Title lengths

The number of words used in each title on the website
