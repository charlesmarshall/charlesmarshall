MG

A classic UK car manufacturer known worldwide for their 2 seater sport cars that set the standard for all others. Unfortunately it fell from grace during the early 2000s with many issues and finally disappeared in 2005.

The brand and assets were bought up by Chinese giant SIAC and have now been used to create the new incarnation, MG Motor.

Back in 2012 we started work on their new website and have since done various other projects for them.
